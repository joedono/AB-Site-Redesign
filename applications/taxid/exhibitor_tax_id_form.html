Exhibitor Tax ID Submission

Please fill out the fields below to submit your Massachusetts DOR Tax ID as an exhibitor for the Anime Boston 2020.

Please Note:
This Massachusetts DOR Tax ID submission does not guarantee space in the Dealers' Room, Artists' Alley, or any other Exhibitor space for Anime Boston 2020. You must complete all other required registration processes.

Each person or company selling their own products at your space must submit their Massachusetts State Sales Tax ID separately.

If you submit your Tax ID but ultimately do not participate as an Exhibitor at Anime Boston 2020, please contact the Director of Exhibits to have your Exhibitor Tax ID submission deleted. Otherwise it will be reported to the Massachusetts DOR.

* = Required information

Please enter your personal name or company name as registered with the MA Dept of Revenue.
OR
The 9-digit Sales Tax ID (EIN) as provided to you by the MA Dept of Revenue.
Numbers only, no spaces or dashes.
