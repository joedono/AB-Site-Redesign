Staff Policies

Welcome to Anime Boston 2020 staff!

As a member of Anime Boston's staff, you also represent Anime Boston's parent corporation, The New England Anime Society, Inc. (NEAS). The NEAS Board of Directors have crafted a set of operational policies to ensure our legal and ethical fidelity.

You are required to acknowledge these policies to complete your staff registration process for this year. Failure to do so may result in being dismissed from Anime Boston staff.

If you have any questions or concerns about these policies, please contact the NEAS President at president@neanime.org or the Anime Boston Chair at chair@animeboston.com.

It is necessary that you read and be aware of these following policies:

  • Volunteer Policy
  • Harassment Policy
  • Whistleblower Policy

These following policies may apply to you, dependent on your position and responsibilities:

  • Conflict of Interest Policy
  • Contract Review Policy
  • Document Retention Policy
  • Email Use Policy
  • Joint Venture Policy
  • Travel Policy
  • Vendor Gifts Policy
  • Written Information Security Policy

The following policies are the by-laws of the convention, and apply to everyone, regardless of position

  • Anime Boston By-Laws

While there's nothing confidential within, please remember these are internal policies only. They should not be shared with anyone outside NEAS or Anime Boston. If an external party asks for a policy, please direct them email president@neanime.org.
